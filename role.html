Your Role in Starshot

At Starshot, we believe that every individual has a unique and valuable role to play in our journey. Whether you’re a creator, a developer, a player, or a supporter, your involvement is crucial to our success. Together, we’re building more than just a brand—we’re crafting an experience where creativity, passion, and community thrive.

Your Contributions Matter

Your ideas, feedback, and actions directly contribute to Starshot’s growth and innovation. As a community-driven platform, we’re always open to new suggestions and concepts. Whether you’re designing graphics, suggesting features, or simply participating in events, your contributions help us evolve.

Join Our Creative Community

At Starshot, we encourage everyone to get involved and bring their creativity to the table. If you're a designer, developer, or someone with a passion for gaming, there’s a place for you here. Through collaboration and teamwork, we can push the boundaries of what’s possible and create something truly unique.

Even if you're not into development, you can still make a huge impact. Join us on Discord, share your thoughts, and help us shape Starshot's direction with your voice. The Starshot community is built on the contributions of passionate individuals who want to see something great come to life.

What Role Will You Play?

There are many ways to contribute to Starshot’s growth. Whether you want to become a moderator, help us with testing, create content, or even design graphics, you can play a key role in shaping the future of Starshot. Here are a few of the roles you can get involved in:

Game Tester – Playtest upcoming features and help us identify bugs before they’re released.

Content Creator – Create videos, articles, or tutorials that showcase Starshot to the world.

Graphic Designer – Contribute to the visual identity of Starshot by creating logos, banners, and other assets.

Community Moderator – Ensure our community remains friendly, respectful, and welcoming for everyone.

Developer – Help build new features, improve gameplay, and develop custom plugins or modifications.

No matter your skill set, there is always a role for you to play in making Starshot the best it can be.

Get Involved Today!

Ready to make an impact? Join us today, contribute your ideas, and become part of our amazing community. Whether you’re a seasoned professional or a passionate fan, your role in Starshot is invaluable. We can’t wait to see what we can create together!
